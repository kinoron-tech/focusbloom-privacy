01 — Overview

🌸 Who We Are

FocusBloom ("we", "us", or "our") creates ADHD-friendly digital planner products sold via Payhip. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you purchase or use our products.

We are an independent creator business. We do not operate as a corporation, and we are committed to transparent, minimal data practices.

02 — Data Collection

📋 What We Collect

We collect very limited information, and only what is necessary to deliver our products:

  • Purchase information (name, email address) — processed by Payhip and PayPal, not stored directly by us
  • Download activity — to deliver your purchased files
  • Voluntary contact — if you reach out to us by email
We do NOT collect: Browsing history, device identifiers, location data, or any sensitive personal information. We do not use tracking cookies on our own pages.
03 — Usage

🔒 How We Use Your Data

Your information is used only to:

  • Deliver your purchased digital products
  • Send order confirmation and download links
  • Respond to customer support inquiries
  • Comply with legal obligations if required

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

04 — Third Parties

🔗 Third-Party Services

To operate our store, we use the following trusted services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Payhip — our storefront platform (payhip.com/privacy)
  • PayPal — payment processing (paypal.com/privacy)
  • Pinterest — marketing and content discovery (pinterest.com/privacy)
Pinterest API usage: We use the Pinterest API to automate publishing of our own content (pins). We do not collect or process any Pinterest user data from third parties.
05 — Your Rights

✋ Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of any future communications from us

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.

06 — Updates
